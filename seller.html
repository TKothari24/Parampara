Photo & Listing Guidelines
  • Use bright, natural light and a clean background.

FAQs
Is there any listing fee?

No, applying and listing is free. Platform fees apply only on successful sales.

What products are allowed?

Handcrafted, traditional, regional, and festival‑related products that respect cultural authenticity.

How long does approval take?

Usually 1–3 working days, depending on review volume.
