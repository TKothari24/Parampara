Celebrating India’s Heritage, One Craft at a Time

Parampara connects you directly with Indian artisans through curated festival and regional collections — making cultural knowledge easier to access for younger generations and giving local makers a fair digital showcase. Discover authentic crafts, support livelihoods, and bring home stories woven over centuries.

100% Authentic Crafts Sustainable & Ethical

Our Mission

To preserve India’s living traditions by empowering artisans and giving you access to authentic, beautifully crafted products. We highlight regional specialties through Festival Specials and State Specials, keeping culture accessible and thriving.

As modernization accelerates, customs risk fading and authentic goods are often replaced by mass‑market alternatives. Cultural knowledge remains scattered and hard to navigate, while many artisans — skilled in crafts, weaving, painting, crochet, and traditional foods — struggle to reach large platforms. Parampara brings these threads together into one place that blends culture, learning, and authentic commerce so heritage isn’t lost amidst change.

Our Pillars

Fair & Transparent Ethical sourcing and fair pricing that benefits artisans first.
Authenticity Verified products with regional identity and timeless techniques.
